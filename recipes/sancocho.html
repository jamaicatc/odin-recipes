Sancocho

Description

Sancocho is a type of Colombian soup. It is one of the most famous typical dishes of Colombia.

Ingredients

  • Papa
  • Platano
  • Carne
  • Especias

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Cut and prepare the yuca, plantain, potato, corn on the cob, meat (chicken, beef, pork, or a mix), onion, garlic, cilantro, salt, and seasonings.
  2. Cook the meat: In a large pot, boil the meat with water, salt, onion, and garlic until tender.
  3. Add the root vegetables: Add the corn on the cob, potato, plantain, and yuca in that order according to their cooking time.
  4. Season well: Add seasonings, cilantro, and adjust the salt to taste.
  5. Let it thicken and serve: Cook over medium heat until everything is tender and the broth is thick; serve hot with rice and avocado.
